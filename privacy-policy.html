Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025
Short version: WorkoutNow does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no tracking.

This Privacy Policy describes how WorkoutNow ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

WorkoutNow does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Health or fitness data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Payment information

2. How the App Works

When you enter a workout request, it is sent to an AI language model (via a secure proxy server) solely to generate your workout plan. This request:

  • Is not stored on any server
  • Is not associated with your identity
  • Is not used for training AI models
  • Is processed in real-time and immediately discarded

Your workout plan is displayed only on your device and is never saved to any external database.

3. Third-Party Services

WorkoutNow uses the following third-party services:

  • Groq API — AI language model to generate workout plans. Requests are stateless and not linked to personal identifiers. Groq's privacy policy: groq.com/privacy-policy
  • Google Fonts — Font files loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect standard web request logs. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or data brokers.

4. Data Storage

WorkoutNow may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your proxy server URL (if configured), stored in browser localStorage
  • No other data is stored locally or remotely

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or app data.

5. Children's Privacy

WorkoutNow does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data from any user, our app is safe for users of all ages in terms of data privacy.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will address the concern promptly.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored data by clearing browser/app storage
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns

7. Security

All communication between the app and the AI service is conducted over HTTPS (encrypted). Since we do not store personal data, there is no database of user information that could be compromised.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of WorkoutNow after changes to this policy constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
